## Overview

SmartClaim AI is an enterprise-grade multimodal AI platform for industrial incident management. It combines cutting-edge artificial intelligence with robust workflow management to streamline the entire lifecycle of workplace incidents.

### Key Features

- **Multimodal Input**: Accept text, images, audio, and documents
- **AI Classification**: Automatic categorization and priority assignment
- **Visual Analysis**: Vision Language Model for image evidence
- **Predictive SLA**: AI-powered resolution time prediction
- **Role-Based Access**: Worker, Manager, and Admin roles
- **AI Chat Assistant**: Context-aware conversational support
